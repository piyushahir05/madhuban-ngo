About Madhuban

Founder President: Mr. Madhuri Gaikwad

Madhuban Mentally and Physically Handicapped Social Organization, Pune works for the education and rehabilitation of mentally and physically disabled children and adults. The organization provides services to all types of disabled individuals from infancy to adulthood, including those with blindness, deafness, cerebral palsy, and multiple disabilities. Madhuban is a registered trust committed to providing services for the welfare of disabled people, keeping in mind social stigma and the special needs of this community.
